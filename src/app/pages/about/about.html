About Smart Life

Welcome to Smart Life — your personal smart shopping companion. We help you discover the right products for your lifestyle, weather, and daily mood — all through one simple, intelligent dashboard.

Live Weather Experience

Smart Life adapts to your location — it detects the weather around you and updates your homepage instantly.

  • See the current weather based on your city or GPS.
  • Get mood-based product recommendations that fit your day.
  • If it's rainy, we’ll show you cozy and practical items. If it’s sunny, expect fresh, outdoor picks!

This feature helps you make smarter choices — from what you wear, to what you buy — because your environment matters.
